INFOTECH


Infotech is a blend of "information" and "technology," commonly used to refer to the field encompassing the development, implementation, and management of computer-based information systems. It's a shorthand for the industry that deals with data management, software development, networking, and the overall technology infrastructure that businesses and organizations rely on to operate efficiently.
